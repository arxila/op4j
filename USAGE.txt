 
 Using javaruntype from Maven 2+
 -------------------------------


 To use it in your Maven 2+ applications, you will need to add it as
 a dependency with:

     <dependency>
       <groupId>io.arxila</groupId>
       <artifactId>op4j</artifactId>
       <version>{version}</version>
       <scope>compile</scope>
     </dependency>
